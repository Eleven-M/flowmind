| Sales (Enterprise) | $5,000 | $25,000 | 12 mo |

### Lifetime Value (LTV)

**Assumptions:**
- Average contract: $500/month
- Gross margin: 85%
- Churn rate: 3% monthly
- Expansion: 20% annual

**Calculation:**
```
LTV = ARPU × Gross Margin × (1 / Churn Rate)
LTV = $500 × 0.85 × (1 / 0.03)
LTV = $14,167
```
